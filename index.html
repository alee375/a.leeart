Let's Create!

a.leeart is committed to spreading art all over the world to anyone no matter their age or skill level. We work with incredible artists everyday to create art for everyone. Our projects focus on fostering loving communities and we do our best to give back to those communities. With our offices in New York, Paris, Tokyo, Los Angeles, and San Luis Obispo, we are always seeking talent and thinking of new and innovative ideas.
